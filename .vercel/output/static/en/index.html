Why does it work?

We believe it is possible to talk about climate without scaring people and using incomprehensible graphs. Our approach is based on dialogue and facts.

Equal approach

We are students, just like our audience. We don't lecture from a position of authority, but discuss as peers. We break down barriers and stage fright.

Verified information

Our materials are not based on impressions. We draw on verified scientific studies and data (IPCC, Climate Facts) and translate them into human language.

Interactivity

No monologues. We use games, workshops, and discussions. We want students to figure things out for themselves and form their own opinions.
