We can live on this planet because of the breathable atmosphere. Sadly, we pollute the air, through which we damage ourselves and other animals. Why? How to get out of this? You will find out below. 

## Sources of air pollution


Our country has its air polluted mainly by burning **fossil fuels**, for heating and transport above all. Many people still heats with brown or black coal. Burning both these fuels is a cause of two main problems:

1. The first one is greenhouse gas CO2, which causes climate change, together with other gases. 
2. The second problém is air pollution in the source's close vicinity. Among other things, there are microparticles gathering dense substances that release into organism in case of breathing. 


Moreover, dangerous nitrogen oxide and sulfur oxide from coal emerge. All of this causes breathing and heart issues, and increases risk of cancer. 

## Coal-fired power plants and local steam engine


There are two main sources of air-pollution by burning coal. First, there are **coal-fired plants**, , where strict rules apply, desulfurization happens and other measures resulting in decreasing of air pollution. The second are individuals - **people that heat their homes the "wrong" way**.

The harmfulness of such heating can be measured by here mentioned microparticles per cubic meter. While it's possible to measure maximum of 300 particles per cubic meter in the area above Czech coal plant chimney, there can be up to 700 particles per cubic meter above a badly heated family house chimney. 


The amount of harmful particles during burning is affected by several factors: 

- Boiler type
- Fuel
- Burning effectivity (i.e., at what temperature and airflow the particular fuel burns)
- and more.

## Subsidies and future of heating

Some people can't afford to buy a new boiler, but there is a programme of so called **boiler grants** that helps in these kinds of cases - They allow e.g. replacing of old boiler with a new one, biomass for instance.

This solution is easy and although not free, the replace is worth it eventually. Moreover, since 2025, old boiler owners face heavy fines. So let's not delay and change our heating already.
