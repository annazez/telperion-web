In the past, people could throw away every piece of garbage to the ground, because these things could biodegrade in nature. Today we are living in the plastic age and we can't do this anymore. So, how can we do this today? And what is the problem? This, and much more you will find out below. 


The issue of waste is a big one and not nearly as difficult. It's just necessary to change our perception. **In fact, waste is a rare commodity**, that is possible to recycle, compost or use in another way. 

However, for that to work out, everyone needs to involve: we can raise awareness, people should recycle at home, towns should reserve space for dumpsters (or dumpsters themselves), private companies can dispose and sort the waste, other companies can reuse it. These things partly happen already, it's known how to sort and recycle the waste correctly. However, there are still some imperfections that could be improved. Above all, it's the recycling itself. Many people don't know both how to recycle properly and how to deal with waste in general. 


## Most common mistakes and tips for dealing with waste


- he best thing is to **use the product repeatedly** (e.g., glass bottle).
- When a piece of waste consists of multiple parts, it's necessary to separate them. Yogurt cup consists of plastic cup (belongs to plastic) and aluminium lid (belongs to metal).
- Liquid packaging board (milk, juice,...) have plastic lids that belong to plastic, and there are special containers for those boards. 
- It's prohibited to pour burnt oil to the sink, for the oil can pollute water throughout the sewer system, even that one has its own container. 
- Similarly bathing with sunscreen on can pollute water.
- Organic waste (e.g., kitchen leftovers) belong to compost, and when living in a flat you can use worm composter.
- Yogurt can be easily made in yogurt maker - no need to buy them in plastic cups. 
- Put the used clothes to the clothing donation bin.
- Dangerous waste also has its container, the expired medication can be returned to pharmacy.
- Larger used appliances can be taken do recycling centre.
- Second-hand clothing isn't shame. Much water ad more is used for production of new clothes.
- Buy food from local farmers (fruit, vegetables, dairy).
- Buy without packaging and to a repeatedly used jar or bag (e.g., cloth bag). There are more and more of these stores, _Bezobalu.cz_ for example.

## The impact of our times

For ages, people had littered around everything they would no longer need, because everything was organic and could biodegrate soon. Nowadays we are living in a plastic age and **plastic are biodegrating up to several hundred years**.

People don't realize that plastic bottles can't be littered around anymore. Millions of tons of waste end up at landfills and oceans, which has enormous impact on how virtually every ekosystem works. Let's do it and together we can improve it!
