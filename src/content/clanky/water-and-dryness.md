Water, liquid enabling us to live on our planet. No life could ever come into being.
Despite all that we get rid of it and waste it. Why? How is that? How to improve it and possibly solve it? We try to find anwers to each of these questions in this article. 

## Water retention in landscape


Water is an issue that we needs to be seriously addressed. There are several problems with that. One of the major one is water retention in landscape. 

Since ancient times people have been draining landscape. The main reason were floods and later agriculture. People have built concrete channels for water discharge, built dams and cut down forests that retain water. At the same time, temperature had risen, so more water would be evaporated and it wouldn't rain regularly.

## Drought isn't just a problem of distant lands

For these reasons we have a major problem here: In 2018 Czechia had the worst drought over the past 500 years. In some regions it was so bad that no agricultural crops grew there and tankers with drinking water need to be brought there because wells dried up. 

Drought is not just a Czech issue. These problems happen all around the world, with the only difference that we, as the first-world country, can handle it. The developing countries are unable to resist, so drought can cause even hunger there.

## What can we do?

Water flows away from our countryside which also decreases groundwater levels. We have also troubles with drinking water that we waste, for example, on flushing.

It's very important to take care of water. Our help may seem beyond our capabilities but there are several easy principles for every one of us to involve in the solution.
